Time Management: Capturing The Golden Deer
  • April 22, 2020
  • Sodt Skills

Time management is the method of planning and applying conscious control of time consumed on specific actions, especially to increase effectiveness, performance and productivity.
Time management skill allows us to achieve more in less time, which provides us with more free time. Having some free time daily is very important. It lets us take advantage of learning opportunities, lowers our stress, improves our focus and helps us to lead a more successful life. Each benefit of time management improves another aspect of our life.
